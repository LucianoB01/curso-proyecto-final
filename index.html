Habitación Suite

The rooms, with simple lines and modern design, tell of the close link between spaces, colors, light, materials, ergonomics and well-being of the mind. The main material is wood: pine, known for improving the quality of sleep. Natural properties contribute to the improvement of psychophysical balance. Room in Stone Pine with views on to the internal street. Bathroom with shower. Features: WIFI, TV, AC, Minibar, Telephone, Safe, Hairdryer.

Habitación VIP

Room in Stone Pine with views on to the internal street. Bathroom with shower.
